damage_notifier
/datum/component/damage_notifier

Damage Notifier Component

Displays clean damage/healing reports in admin chat for tagged mobs. Only active for carbons tagged via the Tag Datum system. Shows damage type, amount, bodypart (for limbs), and resulting health.
